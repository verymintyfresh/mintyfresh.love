Project Mnemosyne

  • Retro-style dungeon crawler made in Godot
  • Descriptive combat system
  • Extensive world-building and narrative

Inspired by classics such as Shin Megami Tensei and Wizardry, with an emphasis on the strange and arcane taken from games such as Cruelty Squad and Fallen London, this is a fourth-wall breaking dungeon crawler with a modular map creation engine and a science-fiction story very loosely based on the myth of the nine classical Greek Muses.

This game is a major closed-source project and will likely take years of development, but progress can be followed through my personal Discord server.
